Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective Date: May 2026. Last updated: May 3, 2026.

Cancellation Charges

  • 30+ days before departure: 10% of total package cost
  • 15–30 days before departure: 25% of total package cost
  • 7–15 days before departure: 50% of total package cost
  • 3–7 days before departure: 75% of total package cost
  • Less than 3 days / No-show: 100% of total package cost

Refund Processing

  • Refunds are processed within 7–14 business days after cancellation confirmation.
  • Refunds are credited to the original payment method.
  • Flight, train, and third-party service cancellation charges are separate and as per the respective operator's policy.
  • Partial cancellations (reducing group size) attract proportional charges.

Trip Rescheduling

Rescheduling to an alternate date is subject to availability and may attract a ₹2,000–₹5,000 processing fee plus any vendor rate differences. Rescheduling requests must be made at least 15 days in advance.

Payment & Booking Policy

Payment Schedule

  • Booking Confirmation: 25% advance payment at the time of booking.
  • 45 days before departure: Additional 50% of the total package cost.
  • 15 days before departure: Remaining 25% balance payment.
  • Last-minute bookings (within 15 days): 100% payment at booking.

Accepted Payment Methods

Bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe), credit/debit cards, and net banking. Payment gateway integration is coming soon for online card payments.

Package Inclusions

Unless stated otherwise, our packages include accommodation, daily breakfast, airport/station transfers, sightseeing as per itinerary, and applicable taxes. Airfare, visa fees, travel insurance, personal expenses, and tips are typically excluded.
