Who We Are

Travel Architects, Not Just Agents

Tourishq Travel Solutions was founded in Jaipur with a singular mission — to bridge the gap between travelers seeking authentic experiences and travel businesses looking to scale. We don't just book trips; we architect journeys.

Our team brings together decades of expertise in tourism, hospitality, and digital technology. We serve individual travelers with personalized itineraries while empowering local travel businesses with scalable solutions.

From the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas to the sun-kissed beaches of the Maldives, from the cultural richness of Rajasthan to the futuristic skylines of Dubai — we curate extraordinary travel experiences.
